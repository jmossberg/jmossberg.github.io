DriveAndPay – Support

About the app

DriveAndPay helps you keep track of your car trips, vehicles, and congestion charges. All data is stored locally on your device.

How do I add a trip?

Go to the Trips tab and tap the plus button to register a new trip.

How do I manage my vehicles?

Under the Vehicles tab you can add, edit, and delete vehicles.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. No information is sent to external servers.
