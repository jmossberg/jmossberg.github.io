DriveAndPay — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-17

Overview

DriveAndPay ("the App") is developed by Jacob Mossberg. This privacy policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the App — including vehicles, drives, congestion charges, and price periods — is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external server and is not accessible to the developer.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data on external servers, with the exception of crash data as described below.

Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics, a service provided by Google, to collect anonymous crash reports. This helps identify and fix stability issues. The crash data collected may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Crash timestamps

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users. For more information on how Google handles this data, see Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase's data processing terms.

Third-Party Services

The App provides functionality to initiate payments via Swish. This opens the Swish app on your device. The App does not process any payment data itself.

Data Sharing

The App does not share any user data with third parties, other than the anonymous crash data sent to Firebase Crashlytics as described above.

Data Export

The App allows you to export your drive data to a CSV file. This export is performed locally on your device and the data is not sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact Jacob Mossberg.
